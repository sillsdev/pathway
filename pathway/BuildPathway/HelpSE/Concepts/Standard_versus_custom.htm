Standard versus custom stylesheets

  • Standard stylesheets are installed with the Pathway Configuration Tool. These are designed by publishing experts. You cannot change then, except for some boxes on the Info tab.

  • Custom stylesheets are those you copied or added. You can edit these, to a limited extent. That is, stylesheets have more properties than you can control in the Pathway Configuration Tool. You cannot preview sample data for custom stylesheets.
